LineageAI Design Document

A guide to the visual identity, color theme, and iconography for the LineageAI application.

I. Color Theme: "Modern Heritage"

This palette blends the trustworthiness of historical research with a clean, tech-forward aesthetic. It is designed to be professional, accessible, and evocative of the genealogical discovery process.
